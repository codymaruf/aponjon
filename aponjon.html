APONJON Small Agro Project

ক্ষুদ্র কৃষি প্রকল্প — স্বাবলম্বী ও সুস্থ জীবন

Empowering Bangladeshi families through sustainable, small-scale organic farming and agriculture.

🌱 Our Agro Vision | কৃষি দর্শন

স্বাবলম্বিতা, পরিবেশ রক্ষা ও সুস্থ জীবনযাপন
🌿

Home Gardening

Start your own organic home garden — we provide seeds, tools, guidance, and ongoing support.

🚜

Small Farm Support

Tools, resources, and training for small-scale farmers to grow organic, chemical-free crops.

💧

Water-Smart Farming

Efficient irrigation techniques and water conservation methods for sustainable agriculture.

🐓

Poultry & Livestock

Guidance for small-scale halal poultry and livestock farming for family sustenance.

♻️

Composting & Natural Fertilizer

Learn to create natural compost and fertilizers — reducing waste and improving soil health.

📦

Farm-to-Table Market

Connect directly with buyers — sell your organic produce through the Aponjon marketplace.

🌾 Why Aponjon Agro?

Bangladesh has a rich agricultural heritage. Aponjon wants to empower families to reclaim that heritage — growing their own food, reducing dependence on chemicals, living healthier lives, and building sustainable income from the land. This is self-reliance rooted in Islamic principles of stewardship of the earth (khalifah).

Organic Only Family Income Eco Friendly Self-Reliance Islamic Values
